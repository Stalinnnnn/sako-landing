How it works

From match day to phone in three steps.

No app to download. No account to create. Just a link, a password, and the clips.

After a match, hand us the raw video. We pull ball-by-ball data, cut every ball, and tag each clip with over, batter, bowler, runs, extras, wicket type, fielder and bowler type.

We organize and write up

Clips file under Tournament → Team → Player. We write the match report (PDF), build opponent scouting decks (PPT) for your next match, and roll up tournament analytics with charts.

Players get a private link

Every player or parent gets a personal link plus a password. They watch their clips in Batting / Bowling / Team mode, download videos and reports, study before the next game.

Common questions

Things coaches ask us.

If your question isn't here, message us on WhatsApp — we usually reply within an hour.

How quickly will the clips and reports be ready after a match?
For a Player or Team Pack, expect ball-by-ball clips tagged and uploaded within 24–48 hours of receiving the raw footage. The written match report (PDF) lands within the same window. Opponent decks for the next fixture come 24–48 hours before that match.
What exactly is tracked per ball?
15 data points: Ball ID, innings, over, ball, batter, bowler, runs, wide, no-ball, bye, leg-bye, wicket type, fielder, bowler type (spin/pace), and commentary. The data is joined to every clip so the video card title tells you the whole story before you press play.
Where do the reports live? Do I have to chase them by email?
No. PDF and PPT reports upload into the same private library as the video clips. Coach or player opens the link, sees the videos and the reports side by side, downloads what they want. Nothing arrives by email unless you ask for it.
What devices can my players use?
Any phone, tablet, or laptop with a browser. There's nothing to install. Players just open the link, type the password, and watch. Tested on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and desktop browsers.
Is the footage really private?
Yes. Every clip and every report sits behind a password-protected link. Each client only sees the content in the scope you've granted them — a single player, a single team, or a whole tournament. Nothing is publicly listable. Nothing is on YouTube or Google.
Can players keep the clips and reports after the tournament ends?
Absolutely. Clips can be downloaded as a stitched MP4 highlight reel or as a ZIP of individual files. PDFs and PPTs download directly. Once downloaded, they're theirs to keep forever.
What's in an opponent scout pack?
A PPT deck plus a private mini-library showing every ball a specific opposition player has bowled or batted in tournaments we've already covered. Stock ball, variations, pitch map, weak zones, dismissal patterns — each slide links back to the actual video. The kind of preparation pro teams pay full-time analysts for.
Do you cut the videos yourselves?
Yes. Send us full match footage and we'll cut and tag every ball, then build the reports. Or send pre-cut clips with the over/ball details and we'll just file them and write the reports. Pricing reflects the work — we'll quote you based on what you send.
Can I see a sample before paying?
Yes. WhatsApp us and we'll share a live demo link with sample clips, a sample match report, and a sample opponent deck. You can show your team before you decide. No pressure.
